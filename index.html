Mission

To act as a one-stop destination for all students looking to convert their ideas into viable start-ups and to inculcate the spirit of entrepreneurship among young minds.

Vision

To empower entrepreneurship by promoting a vibrant start-up ecosystem and facilitating budding students to develop their ideas.

Events

Buddy Program

Enthusiastic students with promising ideas will be provided individual mentorship from entrepreneurs and will be clubbed with one founder/CEO as the buddy to guide him throughout his /her journey of establishing a start-up. There will also be opportunities provided to interact and network with more founders from Manipal.

Startup Visits

There are multiple start-ups/Industries owned by MIT alumni. The proposed body would arrange for field trips to these start-up locations for better ground understanding, and increased exposure.

Idea Validation Meetups

A platform for start-up enthusiasts to get their ideas validated from seasoned mentors, faculty, and alumni from different sectors of the industry. A platform for start-up enthusiasts to get their ideas validated from seasoned mentors, faculty, and alumni from different sectors of the industry.

B-Model Competition

An annual Business Model competition with the main objective of encouraging and supporting entrepreneurs by letting them go a step further with the formulation of a business model.

Rural Entrepreneurship Division

A dedicated division focused solely on promoting innovation and entrepreneurship in the rural sector to create a positive social impact on rural society.

Camps and Workshops

Our primary role will be to periodically hold camps and hands-on workshops for training potential entrepreneurs to be ready for setting up their start-up.
