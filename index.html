Enterpreneurship Cell

Manipal Institute of Technology

We are the entrepreneurship cell of the Manipal Institute of Technology, and we act as a bridge between students with ideas and people with expertise. We want to develop a vibrant start-up culture at campus, focusing on innovation, problem-solving, self-employment, and valuable companies' creation by creating a network of experts who could mentor the budding students to develop their ideas.

Be a part of our cause

Mission

To act as a one-stop destination for all students looking to convert their ideas into viable start-ups and to inculcate the spirit of entrepreneurship among young minds.

Vision

To empower entrepreneurship by promoting a vibrant start-up ecosystem and facilitating budding students to develop their ideas.

Events

To engage with the students of the institute, we organize numerous events. These are few of them.

Buddy Program

Enthusiastic students with promising ideas will be provided individual mentorship from entrepreneurs and will be clubbed with one founder/CEO as the buddy to guide him throughout his /her journey of establishing a start-up. There will also be opportunities provided to interact and network with more founders from Manipal.

Startup Visits

There are multiple start-ups/Industries owned by MIT alumni. The proposed body would arrange for field trips to these start-up locations for better ground understanding, and increased exposure.
